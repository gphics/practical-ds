## Retail Project

### View dataset [here](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mashlyn/online-retail-ii-uci)

## Objectives
1. Exploratory data analysis

### Tasks

#### Sales & Temporal Trends:
    * Monthly & Seasonal Trends: Which months or seasons see the highest total revenue and transaction volume?

    * Peak Activity Times: What are the busiest days of the week and hours of the day for sales?

    * Growth Trajectory: How has the revenue evolved month-over-month from 2009 to 2011? 


#### Product Performance:
    * Top & Bottom Performers: Which products are the best-sellers by quantity and by total revenue?

    * Product Affinity: Which products are most frequently bought together in a single invoice?

    * Price Elasticity: How does the UnitPrice relate to the Quantity sold across different stock codes? 

#### Customer Behavior:
    * High-Value Customers: Who are the top 10 customers based on total spending (Monetary value)?

    * Loyalty & Frequency: How often do repeat customers return to make a purchase, and what is the average time between their orders?

    * Geographic Distribution: Which countries contribute the most to total revenue, and where is the largest customer base located? 


#### Operational & Data Quality Insights:
    * Order Cancellations: What percentage of total orders are cancelled (indicated by 'C' in InvoiceNo), and is there a pattern to these cancellations?

    * Anomalies & Outliers: Are there transactions with unusually high quantities or unit prices that could skew your models?

    * Missing Data Impact: How many transactions lack a CustomerID, and do these "guest" transactions differ in behavior from registered users? 


#### Advanced Business Metrics:
    * Average Order Value (AOV): What is the average amount spent per transaction across different regions?

    * Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Potential: Based on their historical spending patterns, which customer segments have the highest potential value?
